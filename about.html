Who are we?

Nippon Printing Sdn. Bhd. is a well-established Malaysian firm that has specialised in packaging, design and printing for over 30+ years and counting.

We design and produce anything from cake boxes to plastic and paper bags to stickers. Basically anything that needs designing and printing - so even things like flyers if you need! With some discussions over your unique needs, we can handle everything from design to delivery, so you can focus more on your amazing product.

We prioritise quality and always ensure that we deliver the best end product to your doorstep. Customer satisfaction is of utmost importance to us, and we always strive to supply packaging that our customers can proudly present in their storefront.
